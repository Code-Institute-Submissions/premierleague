Introduction

Have you ever heard the phrase, “Premier League’s Top Six” or the “Big Six”? If you’ve been following the Premier League action particularly over the last six seasons then you probably have.

TOPSIXDASHBOARD enables you to dive into the stats of your favourite Premier League teams and compare them with other title contenders over the years. You can now visualise the journey of your favourite teams in this data rich dashboard and analyse the end of season results and compare with rivals.

There never use to be a “Big Six” in the Premier League before, the TOPSIXDASHBOARD can help you visualise the emergence of teams such as Manchester City and Tottenham Hotspur over recent years to solidify their stake as one of the Premier League’s elite.

AUTHOR

I am a north London based Computer Science / Information Technology graduate. After graduating from the University of Hertfordshire, I was fortunate for having the experience of developing two main company websites for an exciting IT solutions company, Joskos Solutions, as a Marketing Executive Assistant. Not only strengthening my skillset in web development, this role also developed my knowledge within digital marketing and graphic design outside my comfort zone. Alongside this I also worked as a Technician for Dixons Carphone for over three years, where my passion for the ever-growing technology soared. I also embarked on a short project for 360GSP, an IT training and recruitment company, as a Designer / Developer which solidified my skills using the latest industry standards.

Due to the nature of the web development industry which is continuously growing at an exponential rate, I wanted to ensure I have the relevant skills and knowledge to tackle real world scenarios within the industry. This is where Code Institute comes in. I am currently studying for a Diploma in Software Development through Code Institute in a course which is aligned and accredited by the Edinburgh Napier University. Upon completion, I will fulfil my goals of becoming a Full Stack Web Developer which will enable me to integrate both Front End and Back End systems seamlessly.
